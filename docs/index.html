About Eldoria

Eldoria is a high-magic fantasy world where magic flows through every aspect of life. From enchanted street lamps in bustling cities to merchants selling common magical trinkets, the arcane is as commonplace as steel and stone. This is a world where wonder meets danger at every turn.

The Three-Kingdom Alliance

Three great kingdoms have forged an alliance to maintain peace and prosperity across the land:

  • Crestfall Kingdom (Human): Noble and organized, known for diplomacy and military might
  • Silverleaf Lands (Elven): Ancient wisdom keepers living in harmony with nature
  • Ironpeak Mountain (Dwarven): Master crafters and keepers of tradition

The Threat

Beyond the civilized lands lie the Orcish Wastes, where an evil wizard plots to summon a demon army using seven ancient artifacts. Only a brave party of adventurers stands between chaos and civilization.

Campaign Setting

This campaign embraces a world saturated with magic, where every significant action creates ripples throughout the kingdoms. The party operates from Highreach, capital of Crestfall Kingdom and home to the Adventurers Guild headquarters.
