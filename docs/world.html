The World of Eldoria

Eldoria is a high-magic fantasy world where arcane energy permeates every aspect of life. Magic is not rare or mysterious‚Äîit's as commonplace as steel and stone. Enchanted street lamps light city streets, merchants sell minor magical trinkets alongside mundane goods, and even commoners might own a simple magical item or two.

The Three-Kingdom Alliance

Three great kingdoms have forged an alliance to maintain peace and prosperity across the civilized lands. Each kingdom brings its own strengths, culture, and traditions to the alliance.

üëë Crestfall Kingdom (Human)

Capital: Highreach

Noble, organized, and diplomatic, the Crestfall Kingdom is known for its powerful military and fair governance. The humans of Crestfall have built a society that values order, progress, and cooperation. Their capital, Highreach, serves as the headquarters of the prestigious Adventurers Guild and is currently the party's base of operations.

üå≤ Silverleaf Lands (Elven)

Capital: Frostglade (The Everwood)

Ancient wisdom keepers who live in harmony with nature, the elves of the Silverleaf Lands approach life with patience earned through centuries of existence. Their capital city is built into a living forest, demonstrating the perfect blend of civilization and nature. The Silverleaf Lands hold two of the seven artifacts: the Shadowstride Boots and the Aetherstone Compass.

‚öíÔ∏è Ironpeak Mountain (Dwarven)

Capital: Forgepeak

Master craftsmen and keepers of tradition, the dwarves of Ironpeak Mountain are renowned throughout Eldoria for their stonework and metallurgy. Their capital, Forgepeak, is carved from the mountain's very heart, showcasing legendary engineering and craftsmanship. Two more artifacts await discovery here: the Tempest Lantern and the Obsidian Blade.

‚ò†Ô∏è Orcish Wastes

Status: Hostile Territory

Beyond the civilized lands of the alliance lie the Orcish Wastes, dangerous territories where the evil wizard has established his stronghold. Here, the final two artifacts await: the Crystal of True Sight and the Celestial Shield. This will be the most dangerous leg of the party's quest.
