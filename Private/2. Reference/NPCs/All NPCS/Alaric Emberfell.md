## Physical Description

Stocky and robust, with a well-groomed beard, strong arms, and keen, piercing eyes.

## Personality

Meticulous and proud, with an unwavering dedication to his craft. Has a lisp.

## Background

Known for his exquisite craftsmanship in rare and very rare magical armor and weapons. Alaric has a deep understanding of metallurgy and enchantments, making him the go-to blacksmith for the elite.

Works at [[The Dragons Breath Forge]].
